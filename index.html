Tori signature
As per their name, it is the signature and best-selling dish on their menu. This menu is suitable for all, whether youre a ramen newbie or connoisseur. Tori Signature Ramen comes with Ikkudo Ichis customizable ramen noodles and features their delicious chicken broth, chicken chasu, kikurage, a boiled egg for extra protein, and scallions. For those who arent a fan of spices, this dish will give you satisfaction from its savoury goodness! We at Flokq recommend this menu, especially for those who want to try Ikkudo Ichi for the first time. You will be coming back for more!
